NeuralCaddie Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Overview

NeuralCaddie is a golf swing analysis app for iPhone and Apple Watch. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Motion Sensor Data (Apple Watch only)

When you start a swing session, the Apple Watch companion app collects accelerometer and gyroscope data at high frequency to analyze your golf swing mechanics. This data is:

  • Processed entirely on your devices (iPhone and Apple Watch)
  • Never transmitted to any external server
  • Stored locally on your iPhone for your personal swing history

User Profile

You provide your name, dominant hand, skill level, and height during setup. This information is:

  • Stored locally on your iPhone only
  • Used to calibrate swing analysis and personalize coaching
  • Never shared with third parties

Swing History

Your swing results (distances, speeds, scores, shot shapes) are saved locally on your iPhone. This data never leaves your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect any personal health data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking frameworks
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not display advertisements
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers

Third-Party Services

NeuralCaddie does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or cloud services. All processing happens on-device.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your iPhone using standard iOS storage mechanisms (UserDefaults). Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features including device passcode and encryption.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Using the reset option within the app settings
  • Deleting the NeuralCaddie app from your iPhone (this removes all stored data)

Children's Privacy

NeuralCaddie does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences interested in golf swing improvement.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
